Mission causes

At Better Nigeria, our mission is to lead the charge in eradicating pollution caused by improper refuse disposal in Nigeria. We are dedicated to fostering a sustainable and pollution-free environment for present and future generations. Our multifaceted approach encompasses education, community empowerment, and sustainable practices, all aimed at creating lasting change.
